Culture

As previous contingent workers ourselves, we understand how important the feeling of belonging is. Our aim is to create a place where people feel creative and free to pursue what makes them happy. There is no one road to compleating a task. We welcome and reward creative solutions!

Development is a key motivator for us. We aim to achieve great things and expect our business partners to show the necessary level of commitment, not only to the job at hand, but to their personal development as well.

We are one big family and we take care for each other. Which is why all employees have access to these awesome benefits :) !

  • Full medical care, providing support when needed even for a close family member
  • 24 day payed vacation
  • Access to premium training services and options for covering individual courses
  • Free gym membership for all employees + access to Yoga and other classes
  • Brand new equipment, custom for each employee based on experience and achievements

We aim to create a welcoming environment for all employees regardless of color, religion background or culture.

#OneForAll is our moto. We are the driving force behind our business and it is up to us to make it a successful one. Each of our victories is the result of our combined efforts.

Talent wins games, but teamwork and intelligence win championships.
